About Multinational Educational Consulting

Guiding students to global educational success since 2009

Our Story

Our Brief Story

Multinational Educational Consulting (MEC) was established in 2009 to address the challenges students face in making informed educational decisions.

We recognized that many students struggled with choosing the right course of study, finding institutions that matched their academic profiles, and navigating the complexities of international education. Parents often invested significant resources without proper guidance, leading to suboptimal outcomes.

MEC was founded to bridge these gaps by providing expert guidance, transparent information, and comprehensive support throughout the educational journey. What began as a small consultancy in Lagos has grown into a leading educational advisory with a global reach.

Our Inspiration

Seeing talented students miss opportunities due to lack of guidance motivated us to create MEC.

Our Growth

From helping 12 students in our first year to over 1,000 annually today, our impact continues to grow.

Our Company

Multinational Educational Consulting (MEC) came into existence after careful examination of the role it intends to offer prospective students seeking to study locally and internationally. Our business started as freelance many years back, but in January 2009 we saw the need for expansion in order to cater for more people out there, having seen the importance of our services to the masses.

We chose the name MULTINATIONAL because we intend to carry out our student recruitment activities to many nations. We have our business fully registered as a legal entity operated by law under the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

We partner with some selected institutions and other educational services provider from different countries in order to offer students with variety of institutions and courses to choose from, thereby leading to a successful student recruitment exercise in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Cyprus, Malta, Turkey, India, Ireland, Malaysia, Germany, Dubai, Mauritius, Netherlands, Poland, Austria, France, Lithuania, Greece e.t.c. over the years.
