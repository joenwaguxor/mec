Admission Processing

Your pathway to quality education at home and abroad

Multinational Educational Consulting (MEC) plays a vital role during admission processing for students seeking to study in an educational institution (either home or abroad), particularly at international contexts. We are instrumental in navigating the complexities of the admission process, ensuring that students receive the support they need to successfully enroll in their chosen institutions.

Our student applications are processed in a timely manner. However, processing time may vary from one school to another; some foreign schools are able to turn around applications to offer letters within 24 hours while others can take a couple of days or even weeks. On the other hand most Nigerian institutions have different admission criteria, making admission processing time differ from one institution to another. The bottom line is that all our students end up with admission offers in good time.

Our Admission Processing Services

Guidance and Support

We provide advice and counseling assistance to prospective students throughout their application process, helping them identify suitable universities that align with their academic interests and career goals.

Application Assistance

Our Student Recruitment Counselors guide students through completing application forms, ensuring all required documents are submitted correctly and on time for successful admission.

Institution Liaison

We act as intermediaries between students and educational institutions, facilitating swift communication and clarifying any questions that may arise during the admission process.

Next Steps Preparation

Once admission offers are received, we prepare students for the next steps including accepting offers, meeting conditions, tuition deposits, and visa application preparation.

Admission Requirements

Admission requirements vary by country. Contact our counselor for requirements matching your study destination.

Local Admissions

Secondary Level

  1. Complete a registration form
  2. Take a pre-admission entrance test (depending on school)
  3. Attend oral interview
  4. Get professional advice
  5. Deployment into suitable school

Undergraduate Level

  • 5 credits in WAEC/NECO (including English)
  • Good score in UTME
  • Post-UTME screening test

Postgraduate Level

  • Completed HND/Bachelor degree with good grades
  • Institution's entrance examination
  • Official transcripts from previous institutions

Foreign Admissions

Undergraduate Level

  1. O'level certificates (WAEC/NECO) with result checker
  2. High school official transcripts
  3. Reference letter from previous institution
  4. Passport data page (6+ months validity)
  5. Detailed CV (for students with study gap)
  6. Standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, TOEFL, IELTS, etc.)
  7. Statement of purpose
  8. Application fee (where applicable)

Postgraduate Level

  1. O'level certificates (WAEC/NECO) with result checker
  2. HND/Bachelor degree certificate and transcripts
  3. Two reference letters (academic & professional)
  4. Passport data page (6+ months validity)
  5. Detailed CV showing education/work history
  6. Standardized test scores (GRE, GMAT, etc.)
  7. Statement of purpose
  8. Application fee (where applicable)

Frequently Asked Questions

When do I need to apply for admission?

Applications are accepted before institution deadlines. Early applications are advised to secure course places. Complete financial sponsorship discussions before applying.

Do I need to discuss with my sponsor before contacting MEC?

Only if someone else is sponsoring you. Self-sponsored students don't need this discussion.

What does studying abroad cost?

Costs vary by course, study level, country, institution, and lifestyle. Consult our counselors for details.

Can I get a scholarship?

Competitive scholarships exist but are merit-based. We can't guarantee awards. Check with embassies for opportunities.

What institutions does MEC represent?

We have unlimited coverage for student recruitment overseas, working to help students actualize their dreams.

How long does admission processing take?

Varies by school, typically 1-2 weeks with complete documents. Early applications receive faster responses.
