Study in Cyprus

Affordable, high-quality education in the heart of the Mediterranean

Why Choose Cyprus?

Why Study in Cyprus?

Cyprus is increasingly recognized as a top destination for international students due to its high-quality education system, affordable costs, and vibrant student life.

Quality Education

Cyprus invests more in education than the EU average, with 3 public and 7 private universities offering internationally recognized programs, including dual degrees with UK/US institutions.

Affordable Costs

Lower tuition fees and living costs compared to other European destinations, with bachelor's degrees starting from €3,400/year and monthly living expenses from €600.

Cultural Diversity

Safe, friendly environment with rich cultural diversity where international students make up 1/3 of the 25,000 student population.

Work Opportunities

Students can work 20 hours/week during term and 38 hours/week during breaks, gaining valuable experience while studying.

Vibrant Student Life

Enjoy Mediterranean climate, beautiful landscapes, and historical sites while studying in this island nation.

Flexible Admissions

Universities accept students with up to 10 years study gap, making education accessible to more applicants.

Admission Intakes in Cyprus

Cyprus universities typically have two major admission intakes each year

Fall Intake (September)

The primary intake when most undergraduate and postgraduate programs begin. This is the most popular intake among international students.

September Start

Spring Intake (February)

Alternative intake for students who miss September deadline or prefer spring start. Fewer programs may be available during this intake.

February Start
