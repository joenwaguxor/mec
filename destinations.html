Study in Canada

World-class education in one of the world's most livable countries

Education System

Canada's education system is ranked among the best in the world, offering internationally recognized degrees and diplomas. The country has over 100 public and private universities offering undergraduate, graduate and doctoral programs.

  • Bachelor's degrees typically take 3-4 years
  • Master's programs usually 1-2 years
  • PhD programs typically 3-5 years
  • Two official languages: English and French

Tuition & Living Costs

While costs vary by institution and program, Canada offers more affordable tuition compared to other top study destinations.

  • Undergraduate tuition: CAD$15,000 - $30,000/year
  • Graduate tuition: CAD$10,000 - $25,000/year
  • Living expenses: CAD$10,000 - $15,000/year
  • Work opportunities for students available

Study in United Kingdom

Home to some of the world's oldest and most prestigious universities

Education System

The UK education system is globally respected for its high standards and rigorous quality controls. Degrees from UK universities are recognized worldwide.

  • Bachelor's degrees typically take 3 years (4 in Scotland)
  • Master's programs usually 1 year
  • PhD programs typically 3-4 years
  • Wide range of specialized courses available

Tuition & Living Costs

Costs vary depending on the institution, location and course of study.

  • Undergraduate tuition: £10,000 - £30,000/year
  • Graduate tuition: £12,000 - £35,000/year
  • Living expenses: £12,000 - £15,000/year
  • Scholarships and financial aid available
