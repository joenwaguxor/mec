Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I start preparing for my exam?

Preparation time varies depending on the exam and your current level. Generally, we recommend 6-12 weeks of intensive preparation for most standardized tests. Language proficiency tests may require longer preparation if you need significant improvement in your skills.

Can MEC help with exam registration?

Yes, we provide complete registration assistance for all the exams we coach. We'll ensure you meet all requirements, select the best test date and location, and complete all necessary paperwork correctly and on time.

What materials are provided for exam preparation?

We provide official practice tests, study guides, vocabulary lists, and other resources specific to your exam. All materials are carefully selected to match the current format and content of the test.

Do you offer group classes or only individual coaching?

We offer both options. Group classes follow a structured curriculum and provide peer interaction, while individual coaching allows for completely personalized attention. Many students combine both approaches.

How do you track student progress?

We conduct regular practice tests under exam conditions, analyze your performance in detail, and provide specific feedback on areas needing improvement. This data-driven approach ensures continuous progress toward your target score.

What if I need to reschedule my exam?

We can assist with exam rescheduling according to the policies of the testing organization. Please note that rescheduling fees may apply depending on how close to the test date you make changes.
