FUND TRANSFER

Safe and secure international money transfers for your educational needs

Fund Transfer Made Easy Through MEC

Assisting students and parents with fund transfer to school especially during tuition and accommodation payment is one way MEC take off burdens from off her clients. We have noticed over time that many of our clients do not have an enabled account that money can pass through when paying fees abroad. It is therefore our responsibility to ensure that all our clients in this category and many more are adequately served at moderate transfer charges. Although we let our students know they have the option of paying their fees themselves but many of them usually insist on using our Fund Transfer service in order to satisfy their conscience about our trustworthy services.

All money paid through us is safe and we remit funds promptly to the right authorities. Because of our transparency and efficiency over the years, almost all our students now prefer paying their fees through us.

How can MEC Aid In Funds Transfer?

MEC as an overseas student recruitment agency play a crucial role in assisting students with various aspects of their education abroad, including in the area of safe and secure funds transfer abroad.

We offer advice on best practices for currency exchange, helping students to minimize costs associated with transferring funds internationally. This includes recommending reliable currency exchange services or platforms that offer competitive rates.

We also partner with financial service providers to facilitate smooth and secure funds transfer especially for tuition and general living expenses. We tend to guide our students on how to use these services effectively, ensuring that payments are made on time and in the correct currency.

Moreover, over the years we have established strong relationships with banks and other financial institutions that cater for the financial needs of our students. This obviously helps us while rendering the funds transfer services to them from time to time.

How Do I Transfer Fund Through MEC?

Very simple!

First decide on the amount you want to transfer, then call or whatsapp 07077975931, 08060581501 or 07046736448 to know the exact transfer charges and the exchange rate if you are not paying in the same currency you want us to transfer overseas.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) On Funds Transfer

How can I trust MEC to make fund transfers for me?

MEC is a registered and established agency under the auspices of Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), of Nigeria. If you are in doubt, you can visit our Lagos office in Festac, make a deposit and watch your application fee and other monetary payment made to institutions and other destinations in few minutes. We have been in business for over 15 years since 2009.

How long does it take to get authentication for payment?

After making payment to us, we remit funds almost immediately to the beneficiary. This means that for some payments, you will get an instant authentication provided there is no glitch from network or service providers. Although some payments may take up to 48hrs depending on the available modes of payment at the time of transferring funds to beneficiary.

Does MEC pay application fees, tuition payment to all countries and for all schools?

Yes we do. All forms of monetary payment can be made at MEC to your prospective institution anywhere in the world.

What is the price for recurring charges per transaction?

The charge varies according to region, currency and even mode of payment. If you are stocked with making payment to any institution, we will be more than happy to help you make this payment swiftly in as much as you pay the assigned charges. Kindly send a message on Whatsapp to 07077975931 or Call 08060581501, 07046736448 or 07032835643 to get more information about payment and the associated charges.

Do I need to be present before you help me out with fund transfer?

Your presence may not be needed. All we need is the detailed payment information like exact amount to be paid, type of payment, mode of payment and so on. After having the above information, we will calculate the charges to be incurred if making the payment through us and the estimated waiting time to get authentication that the funds has been received by the beneficiary. After that you can now make a cash transfer to our account and your payment will be remitted accordingly.

Can you help out with fund transfer for clients outside Lagos?

Yes of course. Distance is never a barrier. Just feel free to contact us either by phone call, email or whatsapp and you will be given all the helps you deserve.

Are there any hidden charges when transferring funds through MEC?

Not really. We make known all charges to clients before committing them to make payment to us. By this we are sure every of our clients are properly informed to avoid misrepresentation.

What is the best time to contact MEC for payment/fund transfer?

We prefer it to be done during active working hours (9am – 4pm) daily. However if payment is pretty urgent you can still make attempt to reach us, although such kind of payment may attract extra charges. Send a message on Whatsapp to 07077975931 or Call 08060581501, 07046736448 or 07032835643 to get more information.

What if I need a refund after making payment to MEC?

We will make refund only if your payment has not been processed by our office. In this case some administrative charges may apply before the refund is made. But in situations where your payment has been processed by our office, No REFUND will be made for any reason. Therefore, we will advise you to be sure of all payments before they are sent to MEC.
