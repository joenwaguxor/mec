With over 15 years of experience, we've mastered the strategies that help students achieve outstanding results in WAEC, NECO, JAMB, and other national exams.

Our comprehensive approach combines registration assistance with targeted preparation, ensuring students are fully equipped to perform at their best. From primary school entrance exams to university admission tests, we provide the guidance and resources needed for academic success.

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should we register for Nigerian exams?

We recommend registering for WAEC/NECO at least 6 months before the exam dates, and for JAMB at least 4 months before. This allows adequate time for comprehensive preparation.

Do you provide original WAEC/NECO scratch cards?

Yes, we are authorized dealers for all examination scratch cards including WAEC, NECO, and JAMB. We provide genuine cards at official prices.

Can you help with exam malpractice prevention?

We strongly advocate for proper preparation rather than exam malpractice. Our coaching focuses on helping students develop genuine competence to pass exams without resorting to unethical practices.

What's your success rate for JAMB candidates?

Over 90% of our JAMB candidates score above 200, with many achieving 250+ marks. Our focused preparation on JAMB's specific requirements gives students a significant advantage.

Do you offer weekend or holiday classes?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including weekend classes, holiday intensive programs, and evening sessions to accommodate school schedules.

How do you handle different Nigerian curricula?

Our tutors are versed in all Nigerian curricula (Federal, State, and British-Nigerian) and adapt teaching methods to match the specific syllabus requirements of each student's school.
