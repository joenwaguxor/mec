WHAT MAKES OUR PRIVATE COACHING UNIQUE?

At MEC, we recognize the need for a follow-up and the need to measure progress; and as such, we provide our clients with an assessment form once their classes commence so as to ensure a balanced relationship between the student and their assigned MEC tutor. It also helps to foster understanding between the student or student's sponsor and MEC as whole. The assessment form is also called Activity Sheet and explicitly notes key areas of interest and if there are any lapses in those areas. Where the student has a problem grasping any topic or subject matter, with the help of the assessment form the right action will be taken at MEC.

The assessment is basically designed to check the level of progress made by both the student and their assigned tutor(s). If we sense any lapses on the part of a tutor, we are more than happy to withdraw them in order introduce a more suitable tutor to match the needs of the student.

WHERE IS THE PRIVATE COACHING HELD?

It can be held within our training center(s) or the home of our student or at any other agreed and approved location that is convenient for both tutors and students.

WHAT IS THE COST FOR PRIVATE COACHING?

This question is rather tricky because so many factors are put together before arriving on the cost. As a rule, we use the following parameters to determine the cost of running our private coaching:

  • The subject/program to be taught
  • The class location
  • The class duration each meeting day
  • The class commencement and closing time per meeting day
  • The number of meetings per week
  • The number of person(s) to be taught
  • The maximum coaching duration for the program

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) On Private Coaching At MEC

How Available Can a MEC Tutor Be?

Our Tutors are readily available for you at any time that is fine by you, be it weekdays or weekends or both. Meanwhile, all classes must have been pre-assigned before they are attended to.

Can I Get a MEC Tutor Outside Lagos?

Yes, we have trained Tutors across Nigeria capable of taking any subject in any given discipline, be it for national exams, international exams and personal development.

How Long Does The Classes Hold?

There's no fixed time slot except it has been agreed upon at the time of enrollment. The classes run in a flexible manner and you can always decide on a suitable duration that works best for you or your kids.

Are There Any Restrictions?

There are no restrictions whatsoever in as much as the environment is conducive for learning, with a replication of an ideal classroom.

How Do I Know MEC Tutors Will Deliver To My Expectation?

Delivery is not an issue to panic about; your availability and readiness to learn are key issues. Our Tutors are always available and in good time, so you don't have to nurse any fear of unavailability for a scheduled class. Once you have paid for a class, be rest assured the MEC tutor(s) assigned to you will deliver beyond your expectations; so the ball is in your court.

How Do I Connect With A MEC Tutor?

No need bothering yourself. All you need, is to get through to our office by phone call or whatsapp on 08060581501 (Festac Center) and 07046736448 (Okota Center). You can also visit any of our office locations directly to make one-on-one enquiry. As soon as we have received your payment for classes, a suitable MEC tutor(s) will be assigned to you.

Will MEC Provide Study Materials For Me?

We only provide study materials within our reach at no extra fee. However, it's important for you to know that we are not responsible for providing study materials for most private classes because of the type of students we teach and their area of challenge; students are to have relevant materials in the subject for which they are to be taught ready for themselves and their assigned tutor(s). If a private coaching is done at our training centers and we have the study materials for that program you are running then you can make use of our materials at no fee.
