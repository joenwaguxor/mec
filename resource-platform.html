Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our standard operating hours are 9:00am to 5:00pm on weekdays for all our resource platforms. Weekend and holiday access may be available with prior booking (at least 24 hours notice).

How does the payment system work?

We offer flexible "Pay As You Use" options - you can pay hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly depending on your needs. You only pay for the time you actually use the facilities.

Is internet access included?

Yes, high-speed WiFi internet is included at no extra cost in all our resource platforms - e-learning center, shared office spaces, and study rooms.

Can I bring my own device?

Yes, we encourage users to bring their personal devices (laptops, tablets, etc.) as we have limited computers available. Our facilities are designed to accommodate personal devices with ample workspace and power outlets.

Are there any restrictions on usage?

We maintain a professional, academic environment. Loud noises, boisterous activities, and inappropriate use of facilities are not permitted. Specific restrictions apply to each space - for example, study rooms maintain quiet zones, while shared offices allow for professional meetings.

Do you offer referral bonuses?

Yes! We offer attractive referral bonuses for anyone who refers new clients to our resource platforms. Contact our offices for details on our referral program.
