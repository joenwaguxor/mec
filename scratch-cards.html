HOW TO CHECK YOUR RESULTS ONLINE

WAEC Result Checking

Visit the WAEC result checker's page at WAEC-DIRECT

  1. Enter your 10-digit WAEC Examination Number. (This is your 7-Digit centre number followed by your 3-digit candidate number e.g. 4123456789)
  2. Enter the 4 digits of your Examination Year e.g. 2002
  3. Select the Type of Examination
  4. Enter the Card Serial Number
  5. Enter the Personal Identification Number (PIN)
  6. Click Submit and wait for the results window to come up

NECO Result Checking

Visit the NECO result checker's page at https://results.neco.gov.ng

  1. Click on Examination Year and select your exam year
  2. Click on Examination Type and select your exam type
  3. Enter your purchased NECO token number (the one sent to you by us)
  4. Enter your exam Registration Number. This is a 10-digit number followed by 2 last alphabets e.g. 2310549571AB
  5. Click on check result and wait for your result to be displayed

NOTE:
SSCE Internal (NECO taken in your secondary school as an internal candidate)
SSCE External (also called NECO GCE or the NECO taken as an external candidate)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What Time of The Day Can I Place My Order?

The best time is between 9am to 4pm MONDAY to FRIDAY. We do not sell cards on weekends and public holidays.

How Soon Will My Order Be Delivered After Making Payment?

Almost immediately. As soon as your payment is confirmed, your order (WAEC or NECO cards in any form) will be delivered to you either by whatsapp or text message. You can also transact with us directly from our offices if it's convenient for you.

How many times can I use my WAEC PIN/Card?

You may check your WAEC results up to a maximum of 5 (five) times with the same pin/card details. In order to check further after exhausting the allowed five (5) times you will need to purchase another WAEC result checker's PIN or Card which will entitle you to another five (5) times check of the same or another candidate's result.

How many times can I use my NECO result token?

NECO result Token can be use for five (5) times to check the result of one candidate with the same exam & registration number. After the fifth time, you will need to purchase a new Token which will allow you to check the same or another candidate's result for five (5) times.

Is WAEC PIN the same as WAEC Card?

Yes, they both serve the same purpose. The WAEC PIN and WAEC Card are used to check candidate's WAEC result, as they both have Serial number and Pin number. The only difference is that for the WAEC PIN, the serial number and pin number are already exposed, ready for use whereas the serial number and pin number for the WAEC Card are sealed on the card, and need to be scratched first to unveil the numbers before they are used to check results.

How can I Trust the WAEC PIN or NECO TOKEN I am Receiving From MEC After Purchase?

After purchasing a WAEC pin or NECO token from us, one way you can trust the details sent to you is if you have allowed us use same details to check your result once from our end before sending to you. That way we are sure no one can hack your Pin or Token details even if they are exposed publicly. As long as we have used the details once to check your result, it will make it invalid for every other user who attempt using it. By this you still have four (4) more times to use the purchased WAEC pin and or NECO token.

If you would like us to check your result once from our end before sending your card details to you, all you need to do is to notify us candidate's exam details to us alongside evidence of your payment.
