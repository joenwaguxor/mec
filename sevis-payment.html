What is the difference between I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 901 SEVIS Fee and a School SEVIS administration fee?

Some schools charge a fee per year or semester to support their foreign student/exchange visitor office and automated systems. These fees neither mandated nor collected by the U.S. government.

Who is SEVIS fee paid to or who receives SEVIS fee payments?

The U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) receives SEVIS fee payments

What is the importance of SEVIS fee payment to the US government?

The payment helps to keep track of students and exchange visitors and ensures that they maintain their status while in the United States.

Does the I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 901 SEVIS Fee replaces the visa fee?

No. I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 901 SEVIS Fee is paid to Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP) while visa fee is paid to the US Consulates in the country where you are applying for visa or attending visa interview.

Do I need to pay the SEVIS fee before receiving a Form I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 20 or DS Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 2019?

No, you must have received your Form I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 20 or DS Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 2019 before contacting us for SEVIS payment. By this we can retrieve your SEVIS ID number and School Code in order to complete the SEVIS form I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 901.

Do I pay SEVIS fee for every Form I Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 20 or DS Ã¢â‚¬â€œ 2019 I receive?

You only need to pay the fee for one Form I-20 or DS-2019, except in the case of a person who is applying to enter the United States to participate in more than one exchange visitor program. Pay the I-901 SEVIS Fee using the information from the Form I-20 or DS-2019 issued by the school or exchange visitor program(s) you wish to attend.

Do I have to pay the SEVIS fee every time I apply for a student or exchange visitor visa?

Not necessarily. If you are a continuing student or continuing to participate in the same exchange visitor program, you have continued to maintain your status, and you have the same SEVIS ID you do not need to pay the SEVIS I-901 fee before applying for a new visa.

However, you do need to pay the fee if you are starting a new program or course of study (excluding F students who are transferring schools or changing program levels).

Do I need to pay for SEVIS before scheduling visa appointment at the embassy?

No. You only need to have made the payment before attending the visa interview. For F, M & J visa applicants No SEVIS payment equals no interview. Visa appointment does not affect SEVIS fee payment in any way. However, ensure that your SEVIS Fee payment is made at least 3 business days before your interview at the US Consulate.

Does SEVIS fee payment guarantee a student visa to US?

No. The Visa Officer at the US Consulate where you are attending your visa interview determines your eligibility to obtain a student visa.

Will there be a refund of my SEVIS fee payment if I am denied a visa?

No refund. Although your initial payment for SEVIS was made through MEC; we also process all payment to the Department of Homeland Security through Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP).
