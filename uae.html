Why Choose Dubai?

Why Study in Dubai?

Dubai offers a unique blend of world-class education, cultural diversity, and modern infrastructure

World-Class Education

Dubai is home to numerous prestigious universities and educational institutions recognized globally for academic excellence across various fields.

Cultural Diversity

As a truly international city, Dubai boasts a vibrant mix of cultures, enriching the student experience with global perspectives.

Scholarship Opportunities

Many universities offer scholarships covering up to 50% of tuition fees, making quality education more accessible.

English Medium

Most programs are conducted in English, eliminating language barriers for international students.

Work Opportunities

Students can work part-time while studying, gaining valuable experience and supporting living expenses.

Safety & Security

The UAE is known for its low crime rates and stringent security measures, ensuring student safety.

Modern Infrastructure

Dubai offers stunning skyscrapers, advanced infrastructure, and a high standard of living.

Strategic Location

At the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa, Dubai serves as a global hub for travel and business.

Diverse Institutions

Mix of 5 public and 58 private universities offering programs in Arabic and English across various fields.

Cost of Studying in Dubai

As an international student, it's important to consider the estimated cost before making a decision.

Living Expenses

The average living expenses for international students typically range from 2,600 to 3,900 AED per month (31,200-46,800 AED/year).

Admission Intakes in Dubai

Dubai universities typically offer three main admission intakes per year

Fall Intake (September)

The primary intake beginning in September, aligning with the academic year start. Most programs available.

Application Deadline: May 1st

Main Intake Most Programs

Spring Intake (January)

The secondary intake beginning in January for selected programs.

Application Deadline: October 1st

Secondary Intake Limited Programs

Summer Intake (July)

The summer intake is less common and typically offers a limited selection of courses.

Application Deadline: March 1st

Limited Intake Short Programs

Application Timeline

We encourage you to contact us at least 6 to 9 months before your desired intake to allow ample time for professional advice and preparation.

Student Visa Process

International students wishing to study in Dubai will need to obtain a student visa after receiving admission from an accredited institution.
